Udacity: Create A Medical Image Annotation Job Project

Instructions

Overview

In this job, you will be presented with x-ray images that are about cases of pneumonia in children. Review the images to distinguish between healthy and pneumonia x-ray images.

Steps

  1. Examine the image.
  2. Determine if there are visual symptoms that indicate pneumonia.
  3. Should the image depict signs of pneumonia, mark it as positive. However, if it is unclear and needs more investigation, mark it as unknown. If everything is cleared up with no signs of pneumonia, mark it as negative. Make sure to include a reason to each of your answers.

Rules & Tips

The images can be classified as positive, negative or unknown: 

  • Positive means the image has clear signs of pneumonia which may include a few things: areas of cloudiness/opacity in several concentrated areas or one large area. You may also see a general pattern of opacity that obscures the structure of the lungs, heart, and diaphragm.
  • Negative means a healthy image that depicts clear lungs without any areas of abnormal cloudiness/opacity; there may be structured, web-like vasculature in the lungs but otherwise that area should be clear. In healthy images, you are also more likely to see a diaphragm shadow. 
  • Unknown means that the image is quite unclear and needs more investigation to determine whether it’s negative or positive. 

Note:
Tweets that are purely factual (links to news articles without comment) are not necessarily Neutral – consider whether the fact/news itself is Positive or Negative regarding the business and select one of those when possible.
